Here Are The Top 7 Websites For Free Online Education

Bro , You don't need an Ivy League education to get a world-class education.
There are many online education websites that offer academic courses for a fraction of the cost of traditional colleges and universities, making them ideal for lifelong learners.

  1. Khan Academy

    Khan Academy is a non-profit whose mission is "to provide a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere." Khan Academy offers free lessons for students from kindergarten through early college, covering topics including math, grammar, science, history, AP® exams, SAT® and more.

    Sample Free Courses: Algebra, Geometry, Statistics & Probability

  2. edX

    Founded by Harvard and MIT, edX is a global non-profit that aims to remove barriers to traditional education. It offers over 2,400 courses from top-ranked universities worldwide. In addition to free courses, edX also offers courses for a fee.

    Sample Free Courses: The Architectural Imagination (Harvard), Financial Analysis for Decision Making (Babson), Omnichannel Strategy & Management (Dartmouth)

  3. Coursera

    Coursera has over 35 million learners and offers courses from 150 university partners. It provides both free and paid courses, with topics ranging from machine learning to successful negotiation. Specializations and degrees are also available.

    Sample Free Courses: Machine Learning (Stanford), The Science of Well-Being (Yale), Successful Negotiation (University of Michigan)

  4. Udemy

    Udemy is a global education marketplace with millions of students and thousands of courses in various languages. Instructors can create and teach their own courses on Udemy's platform.

    Sample Free Courses: Introduction to Python Programming

  5. TED-Ed

    TED-Ed shares ideas from teachers and students worldwide through original animated videos and interactive lessons.

    Sample Free Courses: The Mysterious Science of Pain, How Do Self-Driving Cars See, What Causes Turbulence

  6. Codecademy

    Codecademy is an interactive platform that teaches coding in multiple programming languages. It offers free courses and a premium plan for additional features.

    Sample Free Courses: Multiple programming languages

  7. Stanford Online

    Stanford Online offers free online courses, professional certificates, and advanced degrees from Stanford University's schools, including Stanford Law School and Stanford Business School.

    Sample Free Courses: Introduction to Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Entrepreneurship Through The Lens of Venture Capital, How To Learn Math

Whether you choose to supplement your existing education or learn a new skill, it's never too late to become a life-long learner. These online education courses can help you gain valuable knowledge, earn a certificate, complete a degree, or simply expand your horizon.
